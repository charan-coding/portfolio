Research Publications

Contributions to decentralized authentication, privacy preservation, and blockchain-based security.

Distributed Authentication using Self-Sovereign Identities
IEEE International Conference on Emerging Technologies for Dependable IoT

This paper explores decentralized authentication through Self-Sovereign Identities (SSI) and Verifiable Credentials (VCs). It addresses security concerns in traditional authentication, emphasizing data sovereignty and privacy-preserving mechanisms. The work presents alternative authentication models using Decentralized Identifiers (DIDs) and highlights use cases in supply chain traceability and secure data verification.

Authors: Charan Annadurai, Axel Sikora, Dominik Welte

Privacy Preservation with Decentralized Authentication in Multi-Agent Systems
International Symposium on Advanced Networks and Telecommunication Systems (ANTS)

This paper investigates privacy-preserving decentralized authentication mechanisms within multi-agent systems (MAS). It explores secure authentication frameworks that leverage Self-Sovereign Identities (SSI) and Verifiable Credentials (VCs) to reduce reliance on centralized authorities while ensuring efficient communication and trust. This allows agents to authenticate without revealing their identity.

Authors: Puspanjali Ghoshal, Charan Annadurai, Axel Sikora, Ashok Singh Sairam
