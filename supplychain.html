Agricultural Supply Chain Traceability

Blockchain-based traceability platform developed for the DIPPER project to record the journey of produce from farm to fork.

Overview

This project uses Hyperledger Fabric to capture every step of the agricultural supply chain. Stakeholders such as farmers, distributors, and retailers register product events that become part of an immutable ledger, allowing consumers to verify origin and handling of their food.

Key Features

End-to-end provenance tracking
Tamper-evident records
Mobile data collection in the field
Transparent collaboration between stakeholders

Technology Stack

  • Blockchain: Hyperledger Fabric
  • Backend: Node.js, Express
  • Database: CouchDB
