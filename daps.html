Distributed Authentication and Privacy Scheme (DAPS)

Decentralized authentication framework using Self-Sovereign Identity principles to protect user privacy in multi-agent systems.

Overview

DAPS leverages Decentralized Identifiers (DIDs) and Verifiable Credentials (VCs) to enable users to prove attributes without revealing sensitive data. The scheme removes single points of failure by distributing trust across a blockchain network.

Key Features

Self-sovereign identities
Privacy-preserving authentication
Elimination of central authority
Designed for multi-agent systems

Technology Stack

  • Identity: Decentralized Identifiers & Verifiable Credentials
  • Blockchain: Hyperledger Indy
  • Languages: Python, JavaScript
